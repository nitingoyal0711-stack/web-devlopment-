Poha

A perfect, healthy, and light breakfast that is satisfying and nutritious.

Ingredients & Spices:

  • 40g peanuts (mung-phali)
  • 250g poha (flattened rice)
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4-5 curry leaves (kadhi patta)
  • 1/2 tbsp mustard seeds (rai)
  • 1/2 tbsp turmeric powder (haldi)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-4 green chilies
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • Sev (for garnish)
  • 200ml water (for rinsing)

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the poha with water in a strainer and set aside in a bowl.
  2. Chop the green chilies and onion into small pieces.
  3. Turn on the stove and add oil to the pan.
  4. Add the mustard seeds and curry leaves. Let them crackle, then add the chopped onions and chilies.
  5. Cook until the onions turn golden brown.
  6. Add the spices and peanuts to the pan.
  7. Add the rinsed poha to the pan.
  8. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. It is now ready to serve!
